China Visa Application Services

The Bangor Chinese School not only help you learning Chinese language and bring you to China with us, but also can assist you get your China visa with fast and safe path. If you need one, please call or email us for details.


TOEFL/SAT Preparation

International high school students must overcome many obstacles when planning to attend an American college, including English fluency, standardized test scores and knowledge of the U.S. higher education system. Our TOEFL/SAT Preparation program is designed to give students the support they need in all of these areas.


We are offering small groups or individual classes to students wishing to better prepare for their TOEFL and SAT's. Small groups consist of four to six students. Our teachers have experience equipping and preparing foreign students with the English skills needed to do their best on the TOEFL and SAT's. Individual tutoring is also available any time upon request.


Bridge to America

This special program offers visiting Chinese students a welcoming environment and a broad range of services to meet their educational and social needs. Located in Bangor, Maine in one of America’s safest small cities, this successful program is one in which your student will grow and become a competent young scholar able to compete successfully at all levels of American education. For detailed information, please download the program brochure.

Interpreting Services

1. Service Fields:

On-site and remote medical organizations, educational systems, law enforcement agencies, business, and court interpreting services, including ASL interpreting services, and documents translation. Bangor Chinese School has been provided professional interpreting services statewide in many areas for many years with State licensed and nationally certified interpreters.
